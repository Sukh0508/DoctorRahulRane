Our Philosophy

Patient‑First Approach

Dr. Rahul Rane is a highly respected orthopedic and spine surgeon with over 15 years of experience in treating complex bone, joint, and spine conditions. With a mission to deliver world-class orthopedic care with compassion and precision, he has become a trusted name for patients seeking Orthopedic Care near Powai and across Mumbai.

After completing his MS in Orthopaedics and DNB in Orthopaedic Surgery, Dr. Rane pursued a prestigious fellowship in spine surgery from South Korea, where he trained in advanced endoscopic and minimally invasive techniques. His practice blends modern technology with a deep understanding of musculoskeletal health to offer tailored treatment for every patient.

Dr. Rane's areas of expertise include spine surgery, joint replacement, sports injury management, fracture care, arthroscopy, stem cell therapy, and height increase procedures. Whether it's chronic back pain, a torn ligament, or a joint that needs replacement, his goal is to restore mobility and relieve pain with safe and effective solutions.

At our clinic, we believe in a patient-first approach. Every treatment plan begins with a detailed diagnosis, followed by clear communication, non-surgical options wherever possible, and complete support during recovery. This approach has made us a preferred destination for Orthopedic Care near Powai, where quality and compassion go hand in hand.
