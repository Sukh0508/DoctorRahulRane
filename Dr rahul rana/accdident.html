Fracture & Accident

Immediate, expert care for fractures and trauma — from emergency treatment to full rehabilitation.

Fracture & Accident Doctor in Powai

Rapid Response, Lasting Recovery

Accidents and fractures can occur unexpectedly and require immediate medical attention. At our clinic, we specialize in providing comprehensive and timely care for all types of injuries—ranging from minor fractures to complex trauma cases.

“Our approach ensures that patients receive the right diagnosis, treatment, and rehabilitation to recover effectively and safely.”

Led by an experienced Fracture & Accident Doctor In Powai, our facility is equipped with advanced diagnostic tools such as digital X-rays and precise evaluation techniques to assess the extent of injuries.

Injuries We Treat

Comprehensive Trauma Care

Fractures

Simple and complex fractures of arms, legs, hips, and spine treated with precision.

Ligament Tears

ACL, MCL, and other ligament injuries requiring expert repair and rehabilitation.

Soft Tissue Injury

Muscle, tendon, and cartilage damage from accidents or sports.

Complex Trauma

Multi-injury cases from high-impact accidents requiring coordinated surgical care.

Treatment Options

Surgical & Non-Surgical Expertise

01

Non-Surgical Management

For stable fractures and minor injuries: splinting, casting, bracing, and physiotherapy to promote natural healing without surgery.

02

Surgical Intervention

For complex fractures: internal fixation (plates, screws, rods), joint reconstruction, and minimally invasive techniques for optimal alignment.

We follow globally accepted medical protocols, focusing on pain relief, mobility restoration, and long-term recovery.

Compassionate Care for All Ages

We understand that accidents can be traumatic—especially for children, athletes, and elderly patients. That's why we emphasize compassionate care and clear communication with both patients and families, ensuring they are fully informed and supported throughout the treatment process.

Emergency walk-ins are accepted, and our team is trained to act swiftly during critical situations. From the moment you step into our clinic, we ensure your safety, comfort, and optimal recovery.
