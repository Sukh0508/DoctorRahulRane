Spine Surgery

Advanced, minimally invasive solutions for spinal disorders — restoring mobility and relieving pain with precision.

Expert Spine Surgeon in Powai

Precision Care for Lasting Relief

Back and spine-related problems can severely impact your mobility, comfort, and overall quality of life. At Dr. Rahul Rane’s clinic, we offer specialized and advanced spine surgery solutions to help patients find lasting relief from chronic pain and regain functional movement.

“When surgery becomes necessary, we offer cutting-edge options such as minimally invasive spine surgery, endoscopic spine procedures, and laser spine techniques for faster recovery.”

As a trusted spine surgeon in Powai, Dr. Rane brings extensive experience in diagnosing and treating a wide range of spinal disorders with precision and care.

Conditions Treated

Comprehensive Spine Care

Slipped Disc

Herniated or bulging discs causing nerve compression and pain.

Sciatica

Radiating leg pain caused by compression of the sciatic nerve.

Spinal Stenosis

Narrowing of the spinal canal causing nerve root pressure.

Scoliosis

Abnormal curvature of the spine affecting posture and function.

Degenerative Disc Disease

Age-related wear and tear of spinal discs.

Traumatic Injuries

Fractures and dislocations from accidents or falls.

We believe in conservative treatment first — physiotherapy, medications, and lifestyle modifications. Every treatment plan is customized to the patient’s condition, age, lifestyle, and recovery goals. From your first consultation to post-surgical rehabilitation, we ensure continuous support at every step of the healing process.

Patients often report significant improvement in pain, posture, and daily functioning within weeks of surgery.
