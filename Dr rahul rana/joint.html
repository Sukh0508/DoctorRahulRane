Joint Replacement

Regain mobility, reduce pain, and restore your active lifestyle with advanced joint replacement surgery.

Best Joint Replacement Surgeon in Powai

Precision & Care for Lasting Mobility

Living with chronic joint pain can affect every aspect of your life — from walking and climbing stairs to enjoying daily activities. At Dr. Rahul Rane’s Orthopaedic & Spine Care Clinic, we offer advanced joint replacement surgeries to help you regain mobility, reduce pain, and restore your active lifestyle.

“When medications, physiotherapy, and lifestyle changes fail to provide relief, joint replacement surgery becomes a safe and effective option.”

Dr. Rane specializes in knee, hip, and shoulder replacements, offering both total and partial procedures based on your specific condition.

Procedures

Expertise Across Major Joints

Knee Replacement

Total and partial knee replacement for arthritis and degeneration, restoring pain-free movement.

Hip Replacement

Advanced hip arthroplasty techniques to relieve pain and improve mobility and quality of life.

Shoulder Replacement

Total and reverse shoulder replacements for severe arthritis and rotator cuff injuries.

Revision Surgery

Expert management of failed or worn-out implants to restore joint function.

Recovery & Rehabilitation

Post-surgery rehabilitation plays a key role in long-term recovery. Our clinic offers personalized physiotherapy sessions that help restore joint function, improve strength, and speed up your return to normal activities. We focus on complete care before, during, and after surgery.

With the support of an experienced team, we guide patients from diagnosis through surgery and rehabilitation.
